About Bitquest!

Bitquest! is a 2D Dungeons and Dragons Fantasy-esque Rogue-Like Dungeon Simulator RPG-em Up. It takes place in the magical realm of "Your Computer Screen". Bob is a young and charismatic hero who (ever since a 3 hour Red-Bull fueled brainstorming session) has always dreamed of adventure! He travels to the local death cave in search of tressure, glory, and hot babes

Bob's journey is long and fraught with peril! At every turn he must fight off Goblins, Slime monsters and Dungeons and Dragons lawsuits on his quest to defeat the evil Wizard who lives in the local cave.
